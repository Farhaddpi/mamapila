Our story

Recipes with the taste and warmth of Persian grandmothers.

Mama Pila was created by two Iranian women in Canada who have spent years perfecting authentic Persian cooking. The recipes are inspired by family kitchens, old memories, and the comforting flavours of maman and madarbozorg.

Our first product is traditional Persian beef kotlet, prepared raw and frozen so families can cook it fresh at home. More products are coming soon.
