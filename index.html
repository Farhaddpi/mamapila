Our Story

Recipes carried from Persian homes to Canadian tables.

Mama Pila was born from years of experience, family memories, and the unforgettable taste of homemade Persian food. The brand is led by two Iranian women in Canada who believe traditional food should be honest, comforting, and made with care.

Every batch is produced in a modern, fully equipped industrial kitchen in Toronto under Ontario requirements. Production, packaging, and frozen storage are monitored by a quality-control team to deliver consistent flavour and safety.
